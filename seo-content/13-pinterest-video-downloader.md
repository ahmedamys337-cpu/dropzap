## How to Download a Pinterest Video

### Step 1 — Find the Pin URL

**On mobile:**
1. Open Pinterest and find the Pin you want
2. Tap the **share icon** (arrow pointing up)
3. Tap **Copy link**

**On desktop:**
1. Click on the Pin to open it
2. Copy the URL from your address bar — it looks like `https://www.pinterest.com/pin/1234567890/`

## Common Questions

**Can I download other people's Pins?**
Yes — any public Pin is downloadable. Pinterest does not restrict access to public content via share URLs.

**Do downloaded Pinterest videos have watermarks?**
No. DropZap retrieves the original CDN file — no overlays are added.

**What about boards with saved Pins from other platforms?**
When you save a Pin from another source, Pinterest re-hosts the content. The download pulls whatever Pinterest is serving, which is Pinterest's version of the asset.

**Is the image quality the same as what I see on screen?**
Yes — the download targets the original resolution asset, not a thumbnail or compressed preview.
